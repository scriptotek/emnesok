University of Oslo Library Subject Search ß

Welcome to our subject search. Start by choosing a vocabulary from the list below. A vocabulary is a controlled set of subjects. Support for searching all vocabularies at the same time is not yet supported, but definitely something we would like to add in the future.

Once in, you can toggle between narrow and broad search. With narrow search, you will only get highly relevant results carefully indexed by skilled librarians using the chosen vocabulary. Broad search will give you more results, matching subjects from other vocabularies or even keywords from a variety of sources, but sometimes at the expense of precision.

Science: Realfagstermer

Search for scientific concepts. Realfagstermer is a controlled vocabulary maintained by the science libraries at the University at Oslo and Bergen, and is the best portal to our collections. Realfagstermer is a multilingual vocabulary that can be searched in Bokmål, Nynorsk (translation in progress) and English (translation in progress).

Humanities and social sciences: Humord

Humord is the best portal to the humanities and social sciences. The vocabulary also has a relatively large amount of interdisciplinary subject headings. This vocabulary has only Norwegian Bokmål terms.

Technology and engineering: Tekord

Tekord is a vocabulary created at NTNU (Norwegian University of Science and Technology). It is an especially useful search portal to Technical and Engeneering literature. This vocabulary has only Norwegian Bokmål terms.

Human rights: Human Rights Terms

Yes, we have a controlled subject vocabulary just for human rights terms! Developed and maintained by the Norwegian Centre for Human Rights’ (SMR), the vocabulary has only English terms.
