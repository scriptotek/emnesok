Hello there!

Welcome to our subject heading search. Begin with choosing a subject heading vocabulary from the list below. A vocabulary is a controlled set of subject headings. You can also try to search in all of the vocabularies at the same time, but this is currently not very user friendly as overlapping concepts from different vocabularies do not cluster, but rather shows up as duplicates.

If you use the filter 'Narrow' in the Catalog results, you will only find books that have been indexed with the chosen vocabulary, but you can broaden your search to include results from all the vocabularies - including keywords - by choosing the filter 'Broader'.

Science: Realfagstermer

Search for scientific concepts. 'Realfagstermer' is a controlled vocabulary maintained by the Science Libraries at the University at Oslo and Bergen, and is the best portal to our collections. Realfagstermer has Norwegian terms, but also some English, New Norwegian and Latin terms.

Humanities and Social Sciences: Humord

Humord is the best portal to the Humanities and Social Sciences. The vocabulary also has a relatively large amount of interdisciplinary subject headings. This vocabulary has only Norwegian terms.

Technology and engineering: Tekord

Tekord is a vocabulary created at NTNU (Norwegian University of Science and Technology). It is an especially useful search portal to Technical and Engeneering literature. This vocabulary has only Norwegian terms.

Human Rights: Human Rights Terms

Human Rights Terms is a controlled vocabulary developed and maintained by the Norwegian Centre for Human Rights' (SMR) rights. This vocabulary has only English terms.
